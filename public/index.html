SAMAA Registered Site

Our flying site is registered with the South African Model Aircraft Association (SAMAA). We warmly welcome both SAMAA members and non-members to join us at the field!

Important Notice for Non-SAMAA Members

While we encourage everyone to come fly with us, please note that non-SAMAA members fly entirely at their own risk. This includes any harm to persons or animals, and damage to property. We cannot accept liability for accidents involving non-members.
